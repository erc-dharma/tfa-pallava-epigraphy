      1┼Мm. Obeisance to ┼Ъiva! Hail! Prosperity!

      2-9In the sixty-second year (of the reign) of king Vijaya-Nandivikrama-varman, while the glorious M─Бvaliv─Бс╣Зar─Бya,тАФ born from the family of Mah─Бbali, who had been made door-keeper by the lord of gods and demons, Parame┼Ыvara (┼Ъiva), who is worshipped in all the three worlds,тАФ was ruling the Vaс╣нu(kava)с╕╗i twelve-thousand,тАФ I, (A)ridhiraс╣Й, the son of M─Б(ta)с╣Й, a goldsmith (and resident) of a house in the east of (A)с╕╖iс╣Еgan─Бp─Б(kka)m in (the district of) ┼кс╣Яс╣Яukk─Бс╣нс╣нu-k┼Нс╣нс╣нam,тЖУ1 caused to be renewed the Vaс╣нa-(ci)kara-k┼НyilтЖУ2 at V─Бс╣Зapuram and gave to it the (paс╣нс╣нi)тЖУ3 (called) Aс╕╗i├▒ciс╣Яka(с╕╖am), (which I had) bought from Maс╣Йс╣Я─Бс╣нi, the son of Iс╕╖aс╣Еkiс╕╗avar.

      10Mah─Бvaliv─Бс╣Зar─Бya circumambulated the hamlet (piс╣н─Бkai) towards the right and granted (the land) enjoyed by the god, which (Aridh─лraс╣Й) had given.

      11The feet of him who protects this (charity), (shall be) on my head.тЖУ4 He who destroys this (charity), shall incur the sin of one who kills the great men who are permanent (members)тЖУ5 of the assembly.тЖУ6 If the destroyer of this (charity) does not fear this sin, we, (the inhabitants) of V─Бс╣Зapuram, shall pay a fine of one thousand (k─Бс╣Зam) to the palace of the king who is then ruling.тЖУ7

      15Land has been granted by many kings, commencing with Sagara. Whosever (is) the earth at any time, his (is) then the reward (of gifts of land).
