Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-2 Tenth [year] of the victorious king Narasiṃhavarman. 

2-8 Paṉaiyaṉār of Ciṟṟupāṭi, the servant [of] Perum-pāṇāti-y-araicar, the king [of] Kōvalūr in Mīvēṇṇāṭu, fell when seizing sheep (maṟi↓1) [and] cattle.

Notes

↑1. Mahalingam 1988 considers Maṟi as the second part of the name of the deceased hero, that is, Paṉaiyaṉār Maṟi.
