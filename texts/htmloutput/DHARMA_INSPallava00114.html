Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1[Fortune!]

1-3Second year of Nantipōttaraiyar.

4-9 [This is] the mukhamaṇḍapa that Celvavāṇarayaṉ, the son [of] Amaṉikaṅkaraiyar had made [when] ruling over Vittūr.

Commentary

1On Nandivarman = Nandivarman III Pallava. See SII and IP.

5On Celva-vāṇa-rayaṉ, son of Amaṉi-kaṅkaraiyar. See SII.

6On Vittūr = Viccūr. See SII and IP.
