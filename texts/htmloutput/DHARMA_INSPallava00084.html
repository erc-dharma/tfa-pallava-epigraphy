      Apparatus

      1 -vicaya- ◇ -vijaya- MDS; -vicaya- TVM; -vicaiya- DD
      2 -paṉmarukku ◇ -paṉmarukku MDS TVM; -parumaṟku DD
      3 Aimpatāvat(iṉ karē) ◇ Aimpatāvatiṉ karē MDS; Aimpatāvat(iṉ karē) TVM; Aimpatāvatiṉ karai DD • TVM suggests to read aimpatāvatu karai-
      5–6 Ip-pēr-ērik/{(ka/k)}ku-p ◇ Ip-pēr-ērik/{(ka/k)}ku-p MDS; (I/i)p-pēr-ērik/{(ka/k)}ku-p TVM; (I/i)v-ērik/ku-p DD
      6 pērūr ◇ pērūr MDS TVM; vaittār DD
      7 (Unaruma) ◇ Unaruma MDS; (Unaruma) TVM; (ū/u)rārum DD • DD’s reading makes sense, but need to be confirmed from the original. — 7 (tuna) ◇ tuna MDS; (tuna) TVM; mun DD • TVM suggests to read mun- or ain-. The reading mu-n-nāḻi is indeed meaningful.
      8 vaittu-k ◇ vaitta {t} MDS; vaittu-k TVM DD — 8–9 paṭā/rakku ◇ paṭā/rakku MDS TVM; pa / [9*] tārarkku DD • DD’s reading stops here. His edition has thus 9 lines, instead of 10.
      9–10 kampai/yara⟨iya⟩ṉ ◇ kampai/yaraṉ MDS; kampai/yara⟨ya⟩ṉ TVM; DD
