Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-3Tirthy-third [year] of the victorious king Mahendravarman↓1.

3-13[This is] the stone [of] the son [of] Poṅkiyār, [who was also] the younger son (iḷa-makaṉ) [of] Poṉṉi(taṉ)ṉār, the honourable paternal uncle [of] Kantaviṇṇaṉār↓2, he who fell hit (eytu) [by] a knife, when Kantaviṇṇaṉār, the nephew [of] the royal Bāṇa king marched (ceṉṟu↓3) ¿[in] Vēlmaṟutti? against (mēl↓4) Poṉṉarampaṉār, the nephew [of] the royal Bāṇa king .

Notes

↑1. Macīntiraparumaṉ in the original Tamil.
↑2. Literally “Kantaviṇṇaṉār, of him the paternal uncle”.
↑3. Literally “went”.
↑4. Literally “upon”.
