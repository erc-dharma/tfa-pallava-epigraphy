      Apparatus

      3–4 [maṇai]yiṟ-kōṭṭattu / [paḷukūr-nā]ṭṭu ◇ [2+]yiṟ-kōṭṭattu / [5+]ṭṭu VVA • Text restoration based on ARIE/1922-1923/C/1923/61 (ARIE 1922-1923: page 67, appendix C/1923, № 61), which mentions the assembly of Nallilmaṅkalam in the Mēṟpaḷukūrnāṭu of the Maṇaiyiṟkōṭṭam.

      Translation by Emmanuel Francis

      1 [Prosperity! Fortune!]

      1-3 Third year of the victorious king Aparājitavarman. 

      3-6 Hand-writing [for ...] tax-free, by we, the members of the assembly of Nallilmaṅkalam in the [Paḷukūr]nātu of the [Maṇai]yiṟkōṭṭam.

      7-18 ...

      18-19 ...

      19-21 ...

      [unknown number of lines lost]

      Commentary

      Venkatasubba Ayyar 1943 notes: "The inscription is a fragment; the portion containing a few letters at the beginnings of lines appear to have been cut away."

      Bibliography
