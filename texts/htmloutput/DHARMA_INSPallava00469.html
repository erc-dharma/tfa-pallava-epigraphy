    Kāyampaṭṭu, hero-stone

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2024-04-21Z

    Editor:   Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSPallava00469

    Summary: Hero-stone commemorating the demise of Kattaiyaṉ, son of Iṟaiyamaṉ of Tōkkaipāṭi during a cattle-raid in Tōkkaipāṭi.

      Apparatus

      1 (spa)śri ri • The word svasti is obviously intended here.

      Translation by Emmanuel Francis

      1 Prosperity! Fortune!

      2-4 Kattaiyaṉ, son of Iṟaiyamaṉ of Tōkkaipāṭi, became weak (īṭṭupaṭṭān↓1) [during] a cattle-raid in Tōkkaipāṭi

      Commentary

      Probably dated to the Pallava period, on arguments based on paleography.
