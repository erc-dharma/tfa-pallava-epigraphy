Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

01Prosperity !

1-3Fourteenth [year] of the victorious king Dantivikramavarman.

4-8Stone (kal) [for] Cattima Vakilavaṉ Vīraiyar, who fell protecting the village without arm (aḻiyāmai) (for it, that is, the village), while Malaiyaṉ (malaiyaṉṉār) was ruling over the Aṭaiyāṟunāṭu [and] while an army was coming at once (orukil = orukāl?) on Maṇarccuṉai ... (peṉṉai)
