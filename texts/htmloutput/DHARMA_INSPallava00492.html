Apparatus

1–2 (o/e) /[(ṭṭ/ḻ)ā](va)tu • Three different readings are possible for the regnal year: 61st (Āṟupatt’ oṉṟāvatu), 67th (Āṟupatt’ ēḻāvatu), and 68th (Āṟupatt’ eṭṭāvatu). In ARIEARIE 2018-2019, the reading 69th is suggested, but there is not enough space on the stone to accomodate three characters in the gap and obtain Āṟupatt’ oṉpatāvatu.
2 mi(l/y)i-vaḻi • The reading is mīlvaḻi according to ARIEARIE 2018-2019. It is possible that mēl-vaḻi is meant.
5 [?4+] _ [?6+] • The upper part of the headdress of the left-hand figure (represented below the inscription on the stele) comes between these two lacunae.

Translation by Emmanuel Francis and Valérie Gillet

1-2 Sixty- ... year of the victorious king Nandīśvara.

2-5 [This is] the stone of ... having gone ... , the son of Ammaṇiyaṉār, ... of Malaiyāṉūr in Mi(l/y)ivaḻi in the Ciṅkapuranāṭu.
