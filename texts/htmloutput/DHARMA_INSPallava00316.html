Translation by Emmanuel Francis

The unique hero.

Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

Le héros unique.

Commentary

Mahalingam 1988: 715, № 316 translates as “The unrivalled hero”.

This appears to be a biruda of Narasiṃhavarman II Pallava, also attested in IP 54, 60 and 63.
