Taḷavāṉūr, time of Mahendravarman I

Identifier:

INSPallava00019

Summary:

A Sanskrit verse recording the foundation of the cave-temple named Śatrumalleśvarālaya by Śatrumalla (Mahendravarman I Pallava) around 600/625 CE.

Translation by Hultzsch (1913-1914)

Narendra Śatrumalla, who has humbled kings by [his] army, caused to be made on this hill this [temple named] Śatrumalleśvarālaya.

Translation into French by Sylvain Brocquet and Emmanuel Francis

Le souverain dont l’armée fait plier les souverains fit construire,
Śatrumalla, sur cette colline ce temple de Śatrumalleśvara.

Commentary

See also INSPallava00020, a probably later Tamil foundation inscription of the same cave-temple.
