Vākūr (Pākūr, Bāhūr) plates, time of Nṛpatuṅgavarman, year 8

Metadata

Current Version:  draft, 2022-09-27Z

Editors:   Sylvain Brocquet & Emmanuel Francis.

DHARMA Identifier: INSPallava00155

Summary: Grant (vidyābhoga) of three villages (Ceṭṭuppākkam, Vidyāviḷāṃgāṭṭāṅgaḍuvanūr and Iṟaippuṇaiccēri) in the Kīḻvaḻivākūrnāṭu in the Aruvānāṭu to the vidyāsthāna of Vāgūr/Vākūr.
