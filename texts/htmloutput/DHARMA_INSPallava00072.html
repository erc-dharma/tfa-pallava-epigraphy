Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1 Fortune!

1-2 Ninth year of the victorious king Nandivikramavarman. 

2-8 [...] perumāṉaṭikaṉ, for the nakarattārs of Ceruttalaimaṅgala, Uḷḷūr [...] ... (viṭṭu sarvvaparicālam koṭu) [...] Viṭēlviṭuku ... (mammaḷi na) [...] ... (ṇamum pi) [...] ... (m maḻit) [...]
