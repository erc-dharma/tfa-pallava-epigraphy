Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-3Tirthy-fourth [year] of the victorious king Mahendravarman↓1.

3-9[This is] the stone [of] Karuntēvakkatti, the younger son [of] Poṟṟokkaiyār, the nephew of the royal Bāṇa king, he who fell and died (vāṭi) in the buffalo shed (erumaippuṟam↓2)

Notes

↑1. May¡i!⟨e⟩ntiraparumaṉ the original Tamil.
↑2. See D for this meaning.
