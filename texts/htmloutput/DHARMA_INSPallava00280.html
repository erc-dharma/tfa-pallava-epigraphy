Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-2Tirthy-second [year] of the victorious king Mahendravarman↓1.

2-6[This is] the stone [of] Akkantai Kōṭaṉ, the servant of Poṟmōtaṉṉār↓2, he who fell recapturing (viṭuvittu↓3) cattle when ... (yai) seized cattle.

Notes

↑1. Ma yēntiraparumaṉ in the original Tamil.
↑2. Cf. Poṟmōtaṉār in DHARMA_INSPallava00281, same findspot, king, and year.
↑3. Literally “liberating”.
