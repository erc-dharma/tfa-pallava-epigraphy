      Translation by Emmanuel Francis

      1Prosperity! Fortune!

      1-4Twenty second year of Nantippōttaraiyaṉ.

      5-13Kīraippiriyaṉ of the (glorious) Māmallapuram [through the member of the assembly] of Tiruviḻicil ... gave ten kaḻañcus of gold.

      13-30We, the members of the assembly have become those who, with the interest of that [gold], will provide food (ūṭṭuvōm), when celebrati the festival (ceytu) at the time of Karttikai ↓1 [in the month of] Karttikai.

      31-35We commit ourselves to give ... to/for Kīraippiriyaṉ ...
