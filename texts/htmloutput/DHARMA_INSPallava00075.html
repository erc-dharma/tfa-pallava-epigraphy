Mallām, donation of the time of Nandivarman II, year 35

Summary:

Donation of land for a lamp (and offerings?) for the Subrahmaṇya at Tiruvāṉpūr in the Peyiyūriḷaṅkōṭṭam.

Hands:

The puḷḷi is used.
