Apparatus

2 (ṟaṇṇu)-Āvatu • Reading difficult to make sense of. This is a year in the twenty according to RNNākacāmi 1972. This is the first year according to DDeshpande 1975.
8 rārāṟṟū ◇ rārāṟṟū RNNākacāmi 1972; rārāṟṟu TVMMahalingam 1988 • Reading difficult to make sense of. The reading rārāṟṟu by TVMMahalingam 1988 is apparently a typographical error as he gives Rārāṟṟūr in his summary.

Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-2 ¿Twenty- ... ? (ṟaṇṇu) [year] of the victorious king Mahendravarman↓1.

2-10 [This is] the stone of Kuṉṟakkaṇṇiyār, who ruled ¿Rārāṟṟūr?, he who fell fighting/attacking, [as] a servant of Kaṅkaraicar↓2, when Poṉṉantiyār attacked Perumukai along with Kaṅkaraicar ... (pavvatu) and the people of Kaṅkaraicar.

Notes

↑1. Mayēntiraparumaṉ in the original Tamil.
↑2. That is, the Gaṅga king

Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

Commentary

Kaṅkaraicar: rather than a proper name, a generic title?
