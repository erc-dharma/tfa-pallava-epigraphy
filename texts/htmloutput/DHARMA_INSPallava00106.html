Malaiyaṭippaṭṭi pillar inscription, time of Dantivarman, year 16

Summary:

Foundation of a temple (taḷi) and installation of the deity ( bhaṭārar) by Kuvāvaṉ Cāttaṉ alias Viṭēlviṭuku Muttaraiyaṉ.

Hands:

The puḷḷi is used. Medial i and medial ī are not clearly distinguished and are interchangeable.

Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1 Prosperity! Fortune!

1-2 Sixteen year of the victorious king Dantivarman. 

2-5 I, Kuvāvaṉ Cāttaṉ, alias Viṭēlviṭuku Muttaraiyaṉ, having scooped the hill [at] Tiruvāvattūr as a temple and having installed the lord bhaṭārarThat is, Skt. bhaṭṭa. , this temple [...] the members of the nāṭu assembly of the Eastern Ceṅkaḷināṭu [...] that [...] has done for/to the members of the nāṭu [...]

Notes

↑1. That is, Skt. bhaṭṭa.
