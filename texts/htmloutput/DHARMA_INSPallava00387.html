Summary:

The first half of a verse also found twice in Mahābalipuram (tfaPallava***, ***).

Hands:

The delineation in Lockwood et al. 2001: 288 shows a well executed and almost ornate Grantha.

Edition

Text after Lockwood et al. 2001: 288

Anuṣṭubh
sambhava-sthiti-saṃhāra-
-kāraṇam vīta-kāraṇaḥ

Translation by Emmanuel Francis

The cause of ...

Translation into French by Sylvain Brocquet

Lui qui est cause des naissances, des existences et de leur destruction, mais n’a lui-même nulle cause.
