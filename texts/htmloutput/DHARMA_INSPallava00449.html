Translation by Emmanuel Francis

Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

1Prosperity ! Fortune !

1-2Nineteenth year of the victorious king Nṛpatuṅga.

2-6The glorious temple that Ciṟiya-naṅkaiyār, courtesan (cūḷai-kaḷ) of the lord (uṭaiyār) of Paṇkala-nāṭu has caused to be made for Siṃhanandi Bhaṭṭāra of the great Peṇṇākaṭam in the name of (pēr-āl) of Vīra-t-toḻilar (« the doer of heroic deeds »), son (koṭukkar) of the lord of Paṇkala-nāṭu.

Commentary

ARIE on cūḷaikaḷ.
