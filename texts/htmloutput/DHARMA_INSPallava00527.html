Eṭuttaṉūr label on hero-stone, time of Mahendravarman, year 34

Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-7[This is] the way that the dog named Kōvivaṉ has bitten two thieves and kept guard [against them].

Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

Commentary

See DHARMA_INSPallava00284 for the hero-stone inscription on the same slab, after which the date of this label is conjectured.
