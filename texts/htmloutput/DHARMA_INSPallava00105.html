Triplicane donation by Pukaḻ Tuṇai, time of Dantivarman, year 12.

Metadata

Identifier:

INSPallava00105

Summary:

Donation of land by Pukaḻ Tuṇai for feeding the god (tiruvaṭikaḷ) of the temple at Tivallikkēṇi, that is Viṣṇu.

Hands:

The puḷḷi is used. In line 2, the ki of Tamil ākiya is written in Grantha. Further details in Venkayya 1905–1906: 291–292.
