Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-3 Seventh year of the victorious Pallava king Nandi ... ṭukuvikramavarman. 

4-12 Pātaṉār Kālimāṟaṉ [of] Kūtaṉūr, lord uṭaiyaTVMMahalingam 1988 simply translate as “of”. [of] Nanperumāpuñciyūr [in] the Palkuṉṟakkōṭṭam in AṭaiyāṟuThe subdivision of district (Aṭaiyāṟu) is unusually stated before the district (Palkuṉṟakōṭṭam)., fell.

Notes

↑1. TVMMahalingam 1988 simply translate as “of”.
↑2. The subdivision of district (Aṭaiyāṟu) is unusually stated before the district (Palkuṉṟakōṭṭam).
