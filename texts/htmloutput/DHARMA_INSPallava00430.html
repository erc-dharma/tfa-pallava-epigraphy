    Akaḷūr Hero-Stone

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2022-11-07Z

    Editors:   Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSPallava00430

    Summary: Hero-stone commemorating the death of Kampaiyaṉār.

    Translation by Emmanuel Francis

    1Fortune!

    1-6When Vicaiyātittiyaṉ↓1 ordered [it], Kampaiyaṉār destroyed Toṇṭūr and died [on the battle-field].

    Notes

    ↑1. That is, Skt. Vijayāditya.

    Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

    1Fortune !

    1-6Quand Vicaiyātittiyaṉ↓1 lui en donna l’ordre, Kampaiyaṉār détruisit Toṇṭūr et mourut↓2.

    Notes

    ↑1. C’est-à-dire Skt. Vijayāditya.
    ↑2. Comprendre : durant la prise de la ville.

    Commentary

    1 śrī: auspicious beginning or honorific?

    See DHARMA_INSPallava00084 .

    It is not clear which edition is to be taken as reference. Sampath or Dayalan? It is not clear if Dayalan had a fresh reading of the inscription or simply edited the previous editions.
