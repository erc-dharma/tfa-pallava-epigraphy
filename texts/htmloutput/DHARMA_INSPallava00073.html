Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1Prosperity! Fortune!

1-210th year of Nandivarman.

2-3to Nandivarman [...] the ornament of the Pallava lineage

4-5Vicaiyanalluḻāṉ [...] the banner of the Āditya lineage

6-9[too lacunose to be translated]

Bibliography

First edited in Venkatasubba Ayyar 1943 (SII 12, no. 32); text and summary in Mahalingam 1988 (IP n° 73); encoded here for DHARMA (ERC n° 809994) by Emmanuel Francis (2020). The available visual documentation (photos, 2011) does not enable to assess the edition by Venkatasubba Ayyar 1943, as the inscription is too heavily damaged.
