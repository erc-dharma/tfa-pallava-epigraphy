Kūram, Keśavaperumāḷ, pillar inscription

Summary:

Name of a Pallava king.

Hands:

Edition

1‡ [face A]śrī-pallava-
2‡ [face B]mā-rācaṉ
3[3*] māmalla(ṉ)

Translation by Emmanuel Francis

The glorious great Pallava king [...] Māmalla.

or

Prosperity!

The great Pallava king [...] Māmalla.

Commentary

1 It is not clear whether śrī is an auspicious beginning or an honorific prefix.
