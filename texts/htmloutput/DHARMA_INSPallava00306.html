Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1-3 Second year of the victorious king, the king Nandivarman. 

3-11 Vaṇṇakka Kaṭaiyaṉār, lord (uṭaiya↓1) of Tāḻaiyūr, fell liberating (mīṭṭu) cattle when Ūṇamiṟaiyār, a hunter (vēṭṭuvar) [of] Iḷavenmaṟukkōṭṭu [in] Vēṇāṭu, seized cattle [at] Tāḷaiyūr.

Notes

↑1. TVMMahalingam 1988 simply translate as “of”.
