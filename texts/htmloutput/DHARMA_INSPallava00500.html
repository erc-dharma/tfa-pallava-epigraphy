Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1Prosperity! Fortune!

2-4Year twenty-seven of the victorious king Nandivikramavarman.

4-7Ceruvaliyār, son of Nāyakar Ataṭṭiyār, who fell in a cattle-raid at Curaikkuḷam in the Palkuṉrākkōṭṭam (and) in the Maṇṭaikuḷanāṭu, obtained a land.
