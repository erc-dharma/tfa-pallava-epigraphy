Commentary

Mahalingam 1988: 715, № 316 translates as “The bearer of prosperity” and “The wonderful archer”.

These appear to be birudas of Narasiṃhavarman II Pallava, also attested in INSPallava00054, INSPallava00060, and INSPallava00063.
