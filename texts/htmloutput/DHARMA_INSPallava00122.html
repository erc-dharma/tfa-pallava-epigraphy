    Tillaisthānam, donation of the time of Nantippōtairayar, year 10

    Editors:   Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSPallava00122

    Summary: Donation of gold for the upkeep of a perpetual lamp.

    Hands: Medial i and ī interchangeable.
