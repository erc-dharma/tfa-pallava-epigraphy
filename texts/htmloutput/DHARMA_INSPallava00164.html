Kūram, Keśavaperumāḷ, north wall inscription

Metadata

Identifier:

INSPallava00164

Summary:

Sale-deed of land in the Pallava-nārāyaṇa-caturvedimaṅgala to members of the ... (āḷuṅ-kaṇattārs) of Avani-nārāyaṇa-caturvedimaṅgala, sanctioned by the assembly (sabhā) of Pallava-nārāyaṇa-caturvedimaṅgala.

Hands:

Example of Grantha tu and Tamil tu used alternatively for the same word (caturvedi, line 1).
