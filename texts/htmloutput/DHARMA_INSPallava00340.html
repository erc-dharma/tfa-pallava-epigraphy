Translation by Emmanuel Francis

1Fortune!

1-6When VicaiyātittiyaṉThat is, Skt. Vijayāditya. ordered to, the honourable Kampaiyaṉ destroyed Toṇṭūr and died [on the battle-field].

Notes

↑1. That is, Skt. Vijayāditya.

Translation into French by Emmanuel Francis

1Fortune !

1-6Quand VicaiyātittiyaṉC’est-à-dire Skt. Vijayāditya. lui en donna l’ordre, l’honorable Kampaiyaṉ détruisit Toṇṭūr et mourutComprendre : durant la prise de la ville..
